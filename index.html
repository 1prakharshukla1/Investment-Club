Different Investment methods

Fixed Deposit



  • Low Returns but low risk
  • Maximum Returns varies 5% to 7%

Gold / Silver Investment



  • Moderate Returns but Moderate risk
  • Maximum Returns varies 9% to 11%

Investment through Mutual Funds



  • High Returns but with huge Risk
  • Can be done by
    • SIP (Systematic Investment Plan): Investing a fixed amount in regular intervals
    • Lumpsum: Investing the whole money at one time

Stock Exchange



  • Huge Returns but Risk is also huge
  • Return depends on how you manage your returns

CryptoCurrencies



  • Very High Risk, but with extremely high returns
  • Some popular CryptoCurrencies include:
    1. Bitcoin
    2. Ethereum
    3. Dogecoin
